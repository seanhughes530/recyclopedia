Recyclopedia helps eco-conscious consumers who want to effectively recycle by giving you an easy way to search how and where you can recycle any item by simply pointing your camera at it. We've found that some of the most common questions about recycling are also some of the most basic; is this thing recyclable and if so, where can I recycle it? This is why we wanted to create Recyclopedia, because reducing your waste and recycling effectively shouldn't involve hours of research. So we made it simple.

Recyclopedia is in development, check back soon for updates.
